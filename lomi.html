Lomi

Ingredients:

  • 500g of any thick Egg Noodles
  • 1 1/2 cup pork belly, cubed into small pieces
  • 1 Sliced Pork Liver
  • 1 cup Sliced Pork Heart
  • 10 pcs Kikiam cut in halves
  • 8 cups Chicken Stock
  • 3 tbsp Sweet Potato Starch
  • 2 Beaten Eggs
  • 6 Minced Garlic Cloves
  • 2 pcs finely chopped Shallot
  • Oil
  • Ground Black Pepper
  • Fish Sauce

Garnish:

  • 150g Chicharon
  • Hard-boiled Eggs
  • Chopped Spring Onions

Steps:

  1. Cook noodles according to packaging instructions, once cooked, drain and rinse then set aside.
  2. In a pot, add a bit of oil and fry pork belly until golden brown, then remove from pot and set aside.
  3. Brown pork heart on all sides.
  4. Add and cook kikiam for 2 minutes.
  5. Add garlic and shallots then saut√© for 1 minute.
  6. Add and cook liver until light brown.
  7. Add and bring chicken stock to a boil then simmer for 10 minutes in low heat.
  8. Add fried pork and simmer for 2 more minutes.
  9. Mix sweet potato starch and small amount of wter, then pour in the pot and simmer for 2 minutes.
  10. Add noodles and bring to a boil again. If necessary, add water and once boiling, turn off heat and add eggs while continuously mixing.
  11. Season with ground pepper and fish sauce then serve with the garnish.
