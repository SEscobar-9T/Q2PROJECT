Arroz Caldo

Ingredients

  • 1 big Ginger
  • 1 Chopped Onion
  • 2 tbsp Chopped Garlic
  • 1 1/2 cups Uncooked Rice
  • Chicken preferably bone-parts
  • 5 cups water
  • 4 pcs medium/hard-boiled Eggs
  • Julienned Scallions
  • Ground Pepper
  • Fish Sauce
  • Chicken Cube
  • Lemon/Lime or kalamansi
  • 2 tbsp Oil

Steps

  1. In a pot, add oil then fry 1/3 of garlic, onions, and ginger.
  2. Add chicken and fry until skin turns golden brown, then add a dash of fish sauce and ground pepper.
  3. Add rice and cook for several minutes, then add chicken cube and dissolve properly.
  4. Add water then bring to a boil.
  5. Let rice simmer and stir occationally for 30-40 minutes until cooked.
  6. While dish is still cooking, fry garlic until it turns golden brown.
  7. Serve with toppings(fried garlic, scallions, kalamansi) on top.
