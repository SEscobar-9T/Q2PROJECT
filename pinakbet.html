Pinakbet

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb Sliced Lechon Kawali
  • 1 pc Shrimp Cube
  • 12 pcs Sitaw cut into 2 in length
  • 1/2 pcs Cubed Kalabasa
  • 1 pc Sliced Chinese Eggplant
  • 1 pc Sliced Ampalaya
  • Optional: 1 pc Cubed Kamote
  • 2 pcs Cubed Tomato
  • Optional: 2 thumbs Crushed Ginger
  • 1 pc Chopped Onion
  • 4 pcs Crushed Garlic
  • 2 tsp Bagoong Alamang
  • 2 1/2 cups water
  • 3 tbsp Cooking Oil
  • 1/4 tsp Ground Black Pepper

Steps:

  1. Add cooking oil in a pot and saute onion and add ginger until garlic until onion softens.
  2. Saute the lechon kawali for 1 minute.
  3. Boil water
  4. Add shrimp cube, cover pot, and cook in medium temp for 20 minutes.
  5. Add tomato and bagoong alamang then cook for 3 minutes.
  6. Add kalabasa and kamote then cook for 7 minutes.
  7. Add sitaw, okra, ampalaya, and eggplant then cook for 5 minutes.
  8. Optional: Season with black pepper and top with lechon kawali.
  9. Transfer to a serving plate and enjoy!
