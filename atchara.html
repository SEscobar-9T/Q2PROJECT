Atchara

Ingredients:

  • 650 g Green Papaya, julienned (about 1 green papaya)
  • 75 g Carrot, julienned (about 1 medium carrot)
  • 50 g Green Bell Pepper, julienned (1/4 of a whole green bell pepper)
  • 50 g Red Bell Pepper, julienned (1/4 of a whole red bell pepper)
  • 1 tbsp Salt

For pickling liquid:

  • 3 cups Cane Vinegar (or regular white vinegar)
  • 1.5 cups Sugar
  • 1.5 tsp Salt
  • 1/2 tsp Ground Black Pepper
  • 1/2 head thinly sliced Garlic

Steps:

  1. Put the julienned ingredients into a bowl and sprinkle salt to remove moisture, let sit for 30 minutes.
  2. Pour out excess liquid from bowl and rinse the julienned ingredients with cold water thrice to remove salt
  3. Remove as much water as possible using a cheese cloth then transfer into whatever container you want.
  4. In a saucepan, combine vinegar, sugar, salt, black pepper, and garlic and bring to a boil to dissolve sugar and simmer for 5 minutes.
  5. Pour the hot pickling liquid into your vegetables and use utensils to evenly distribute the liquid.
  6. Marinate for 24-48 hours before consuming.
