Chicken Adobo

Ingredients:

  • 1 tbsp Oil
  • 4 bone-in, skin-on chicken legs, separated into thighs and drumsticks
  • Salt
  • 8 Thinly sliced Garlic
  • 2 fresh Bay Leaves
  • 1 1/2 tsp Black Peppercorns
  • 1 1/4 cups water
  • 1 cup Soy Sauce
  • 1 cup Rice Vinegar
  • Rice for serving

Steps:

  1. In a pot, heat oil over medium heat until simmering, then dry chicken by blotting with paper towels and season lightly with salt.
  2. Add chicken pieces to pot skin side down while making sure to not overcrowd pot, then cook for 6-7 minutes until well-browned. Using tongs, flip chicken pieces and cook for 3 minutes until lightly brown on second side, then set aside.
  3. Add garlic, bay leaves, and peppercorns to empty pot then cook for 30 seconds while stirring constantly until mixture is fragrant and garlic is light golden. Then add water and stir while scraping the brown bits from the bottom of the pot, and add soy sauce, vinegar, and chicken pieces back in pot then increase heat to high and bring liquid to boil. Reduce heat to low, cover then simmer while turning chicken pieces halfway for 20 minutes until chicken is cooked and tender.
  4. Dish is best served after sitting overnight in refrigerator or served immediately with rice, and chicken pieces can be broiled before serving.
