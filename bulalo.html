Bulalo

Ingredients:

  • 3 lbs Bone-in Beef Shanks
  • cold water
  • 1 large peeled and quartered Onion
  • 2 tbsp Fish Sauce
  • 1 tbsp Peppercorn
  • 2 Corn Cobs, husked and cut into halves
  • 1 bunch Pechay, leaves separated
  • 2 Green Onions, stemmed and cut into halves
  • Salt to taste

For dipping sauce:

  • To taste:
  • Fish Sauce
  • Calamansi Juice
  • Chopped Thai Chili Peppers

Steps:

  1. In a pot, combine beef shanks and cold water, then bring to a boil
  2. Drain water then rinse the beef under cold water to remove impurities then rinse the pot.
  3. In the pot, return meat and enough cold water, then bring to a boil while skimming scum that accumulates on the surface.
  4. When broth is clear, add onions, fish sauce, and peppercorns.
  5. Lower heat and cook at bare simmer for 2 hours or until meat is fork-tender.
  6. Add corn then simmer for 10-15 minutes or until corn is tender.
  7. Add pechay and green onions then cook for 2-3 minutes or until vegetables are tender.
  8. Add salt to taste and serve hot with fish sauce and calamansi.
