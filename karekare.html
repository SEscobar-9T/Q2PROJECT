Kare-Kare

Ingredients:

  • 3 lbs Oxtail cut into 2in slices
  • 1 pc Sliced small Banana Flower Bud
  • 1 bundle Pechay/Bok Choy
  • 1 bundle String Beans cut into 2in slices
  • 4 pcs Sliced Eggplants
  • 1 cup Ground Peanuts
  • 1/2 cup Peanut Butter
  • 1/2 cup Shrimp Paste
  • 1L water
  • 1/2 cup Annatto Seeds soaked in water
  • 1/2 cup Toasted Ground Rice
  • 1 tbsp Minced Garlic
  • 1 pc Chopped Onion
  • Salt & Pepper

Steps:

  1. In a pot, bring water to a boil.
  2. Add oxtail and onions and simmer for 3 hours or until tender.
  3. Once meat is tender, add ground peanuts, peanut butter, and water from annatto seed mixture then simmer for 5-7 minutes.
  4. Add toasted ground rice then simmer for 5 minutes.
  5. On a pan, saute garlic and add banana flower, eggplant, and string beans then cook for 5 minutes.
  6. Transfer cooked vegetables to the pot where the rest of the ingredients are.
  7. Add salt and pepper to taste.
  8. Serve hot with shrimp paste.
