Pork Sinigang

Ingredients:

  • 500g Pork Belly Chunks
  • 500g induvidual American Pork Ribs
  • 2 Sliced medium Onions
  • 2.5cm Knob Ginger, smashed and roughly chopped
  • 2 Quartered Tomatoes
  • 150g Snake Beans cut into 5cm lengths
  • 3 Red/Green Chilies
  • 1 small Eggplant cut into 5cm lengths
  • 6-8 Okra
  • 200g Kangkong
  • 2-2.5L water
  • 3 tbsp Fish Sauce
  • 1 packet Tamarind Soup Mix
  • 2 tbsp Cooking Oil
  • Optional: Salt

Steps:

  1. In a pot, saut√© onions until translucent.
  2. Add pork parts and cook for 10 minutes.
  3. Add smashed ginger and stir for 1 minute, then add tomatoes and fish sauce then stir.
  4. Add water and bring to boil, once boiling, lower heat then simmer on low fire for 40 minutes
  5. Add soup mix, chilies, snake beans and eggplant then let boil for 8 minutes.
  6. Add okra and simmer for 3 minutes.
  7. Add kangkong and use residual heat to cook kangkong.
  8. Serve while hot.
