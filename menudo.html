Pork Menudo

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs Pork
  • 1/4 Pig Liver
  • 1 cup Diced Potatoes
  • 1 pcs Cubed Carrot
  • 1/2 cup Soy Sauce
  • 1/2 piece Lemon
  • 1 pcs Chopped Onion
  • 3 cloves Minced Garlic
  • 1 tsp Sugar
  • 3/4 cup Tomato Sauce
  • 1 cup water
  • 4 pcs Diagonally sliced Hotdogs
  • 2 tbsp Cooking Oil
  • 2-3 pcs Dried Bay Leaves
  • Salt & Pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Combine your pork, soy sauce, and lemon in a bowl and marinate for 1 hour.
  2. Heat oil in a pan.
  3. Saut√© onion and garlic.
  4. Add marinated pork and cook for 5-7 minutes.
  5. Add tomato sauce, water, and bay leaves, then let boil and simmer for 30 mins-1 hour depending on toughness of pork, add water if necessary.
  6. Add liver and hot dogs then cook for 5 minutes.
  7. Add potatoes, carrots, sugar, salt, and pepper then stir and cook for 8-12 minutes.
  8. Transfer to serving plate and share.
