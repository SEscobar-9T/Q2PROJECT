Tinola

Ingredients:

  • 1 tbsp Cooking Oil
  • 1 Chopped White Onion
  • 2 Minced Garlic Cloves
  • 1.5in peeled and thinly sliced Fresh Ginger
  • 1 tbsp Fish Sauce
  • 1.4kg Chicken Legs and Thighs
  • 2 14oz cans of Chicken Broth
  • 1 peeled and cut into small cubes Chayote Squash
  • 1 ChoppedBok Choy
  • 1 Chopped Package Spinach
  • Salt & Pepper to taste

Steps

  1. In a pot, add oil over medium heat, then add onion and garlic then cook and stir for 2 minutes until fragrant. Cook for 1-2 minutes and add chicken then cook for 5 minutes.
  2. Add chicken broth and bring to boil. Then, cook for 5 minutes. Add squash then simmer for 10 minutes until chicken is not pink in the center then season with salt and pepper.
  3. Add the greens and cook for 1-2 minutes until spinach is just wilted, then serve while hot.
