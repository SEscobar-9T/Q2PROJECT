Pancit Palabok

Ingredients:

  • 500g Rice Noodles
  • 45ml Oil
  • 3 cloves Minced Garlic
  • 1 Chopped Onion
  • 300g Ground Pork
  • 45ml Fish Sauce
  • 3.6g Annatto Powder
  • 1L water for Annatto Powder
  • 1 Shrimp or Pork Bouillon Cube
  • 60g Flour
  • 236ml water for Flour
  • 2.3g Ground Pepper

For the toppings:

  • 4 Sliced hard-boiled Eggs
  • 130g Boiled Prawn
  • 120g Smoked Fish
  • 230g Crushed Pork Rind
  • 25g Chopped Spring Onions
  • 2 Calamansi cut into halves

Steps:

  1. Soak rice noodles in water for 15 minutes.
  2. Boil water in a large pot while soaking noodles
  3. After noodles are done soaking, move them into the boiling water and leave for 3-5 minutes or until noodles are cooked but firm.
  4. Once cooked, drain and transfer back to bowl and set aside.
  5. Pour oil into saucepan and put on medium heat, then saut√© garlic and onion until tender.
  6. Add ground pork and fish sauce and cook for 5-10 minutes while breaking the ground pork.
  7. Add annatto powder and 1L of water and stir in a large bowl, after stirring, pour into saucepan.
  8. Add bouillon cube into pan and simmer until boiling.
  9. After mixing flour and 236ml of water in a bowl, pour into saucepan and stir continuously until sauce thickens.
  10. Season with ground pepper and turn off heat.
  11. Add 3/4ths of sauce on rice noodles and mix well, then transfer on a serving dish and add remaining sauce to noodles.
  12. Add your toppings and serve with calamansi wedges.
