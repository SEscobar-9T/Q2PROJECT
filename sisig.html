Pork Sisig

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb Pig Ears
  • 3 pcs Bay Leaves
  • 1 lb Pig Snout
  • 1 lb Pork Belly
  • 2 pcs Minced Onions
  • 2 tsp Salt
  • 4 thumbs Crushed Ginger
  • 1 quarts water

For sisig dressing:

  • 1/2 cup Mayonnaise
  • 2 tbsp Sukang Iloko
  • 1/4 tsp Ground Black Pepper
  • 1 tsp Salt
  • 1 tsp Sugar
  • 1/4 cup Liver Spread
  • 1 tbsp Liquid Seasoning
  • 2 Limes

Steps:

  1. Combine your pork parts, ginger, bay leaves, 2 tsp of salt, and water in a pot and boil for 1 hour. Then, remove from pot and drain water.
  2. Grill your pork parts for 5 mins per side, then remove from grill and let cool.
  3. Make the dressing by mixing all ingredients well.
  4. Chop your pork parts into small pieces then combine with the dressing and toss until all ingredients are well blended.
  5. Transfer into plate, best served with your favorite drink. Share and enjoy!
